### **Key Evaluation Metrics for Your Company**

When using this table to evaluate a specific company, consider these three research-driven pillars:

1.  **The "Credence" Factor:** In industries like Healthcare or Finance, consumers cannot easily verify if their data is safe today. They rely on "Credence Trust." A breach here doesn't just lose a sale; it invalidates the brand's primary reason for existing.
2.  **The "Affective Mediator":** Research from 2026 shows trust is the "full mediator" of revenue. When trust breaks, the emotional attachment (loyalty) drops to zero, and the company must spend massively on "Reputation Repair" marketing to regain a baseline.
3.  **The Churn Multiplier:** For subscription-based businesses (SaaS/Retail), the revenue loss is compounding. A 20% loss in the member base post-incident represents a permanent reduction in future cash flow that often takes 3–5 years to replace.
